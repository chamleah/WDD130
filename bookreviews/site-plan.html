Content

Home page

Welcome to my Book Review Site! I am glad to have you here! Who else absolutely loves the ACOTAR and TOG series? If not, then welcome to the amazing universe of ACOTAR and TOG! On this website, you will find my honest reviews of each book in these series followed by links that include summaries, study guides, and character information that will be helpful while reading these books. To get you a little more excited about reading this new universe, I have also included a personalized FANART page that will help you visualize the characters and key moments in the books! After reading the books, I also have a MERCHANDISE page that will give you links to go buy some super cute clothes, stickers, and decals with the theme of ACOTAR and TOG. I also have included links on this home page under each book review that will show you where the cheapest place is to buy each of the series! Please email or message me if you have any reccomedations for book/book series that I should add!
